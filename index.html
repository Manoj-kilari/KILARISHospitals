About Kilari's Hospitals

A legacy of excellence in healthcare, serving communities with compassion and innovation for over three decades.

Healing Since 1990

Kilari's Hospitals began as a small clinic with a big vision - to make world-class healthcare accessible to everyone. Today, we stand as a leading multispeciality hospital network, combining cutting-edge technology with compassionate care.

Founded by Dr. Ramesh Kilari, our hospital has grown from a 50-bed facility to a 500+ bed healthcare powerhouse, serving over 2 million patients annually across our multiple locations.
